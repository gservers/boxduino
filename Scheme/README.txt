1. Import PCD8544LCD.fzpz to Fritzing
2. Open BOXduino.fzz

My PCD8544 pinout:
Pin 1 - VCC
Pin 2 - GND
Pin 3 - SCE
Pin 4 - RST
Pin 5 - D/C
Pin 6 - DN(MOSI)
Pin 7 - SCLK
Pin 8 - Backlight
Please make sure Your pinout is the same or make needed fixes in Your circuit.
PCD8544.h will also need to be modified.
